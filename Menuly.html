Menuly

I created Menuly in order to attempt to minimize an issue I noticed during my time working in the service industry: unorganization. Most of the time, menu descriptions are stored on something such as a google doc. The goal of Menuly is to allow managers to easily make changes to the menu descriptions, and to allow servers an easy interfance to referance and study. While further implementation is needed in order to fully realize this goal, the general structure of Menuly has put together.
