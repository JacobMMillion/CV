Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our applications (“Apps”) handle the information you provide when you use them. By installing or using any of our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following types of information:

  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication to sign you in. We may store your email address, display name, and profile photo (if provided) solely for login purposes.
  • User-Provided Content: Any content you create or save in the App (for example, journal entries, notes, or personalized settings) is stored in Cloud Firestore.
  • Subscription Status: We use RevenueCat to validate in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat only stores subscription receipts and status; no additional personal data is collected.
3. How We Use Your Information
  • Authenticate and manage your user account.
  • Store and retrieve your app data (notes, journal entries, preferences) in Firestore.
  • Verify your subscription status via RevenueCat to unlock premium App features.
4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore (Google LLC): For secure user login and cloud data storage.
  • RevenueCat: For managing and validating in-app subscriptions.

Please review their respective privacy policies to understand how they handle and secure your data:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All data stored in Cloud Firestore and RevenueCat is secured according to each provider’s best practices, including encryption in transit and at rest.

6. Data Retention

Your account information and user-provided content remain stored until you delete your account or remove the content within the App. Subscription records are retained by RevenueCat according to their retention policy.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective Date” above and notify you within the App where feasible.
